Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of lasagna noodles
  • One gallon of pasta sauce (homemade preferred!)
  • 2 lbs of genuine nordic sausage (no casings)
  • 1 lb of mozzarella cheese
  • 3 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups of chopped spinach
  • Indeterminate Seasonings and aromatics
  • Probably other things!

Steps

  1. Place noodles in water to soften for an appropriate amount of time.
  2. Brown sausage in a pan with seasonings and aromatics
  3. Combine meat n' veggies with sauce in a large bowl
  4. Oh right you probably should have preheated the oven already. Do so now I guess.
  5. Alternate layers of sauce mixture, cheese, and noodles until it looks lasagna-ish. Make sure the top layer is cheese.
  6. Toss it in the oven until it becomes lasagna!
